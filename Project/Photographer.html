Wildlife Photographer Of The Year

Exhibition now open through April 17, 2022


Complimentary with Zoo admission

The Wildlife Photographer of the Year exhibition celebrates nature photography and photojournalism. The exhibition showcases 100 photographs in a variety of competition categories. The Detroit Zoo is the first stop in the United States for this traveling exhibition.

Access to the exhibition is included with your Zoo admission. This internationally renowned exhibit explores the natural world in all its fragility and diversity with powerful photographs to tell the story of a planet under pressure. Gaze through the lens of some of the world’s best wildlife photographers and marvel at the beauty of our planet.
