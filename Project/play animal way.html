Play The Animal Way

You like to play? We do too! Entertain your inner wild thing in the wacky world of animal enrichment! Watch your favorite Zoo residents investigate the most colorful treats of the year, sniff out their favorite scents, and solve challenging puzzles.

An annual fall favorite, Play the Animal Way is a Zoo-wide showcase of the creative talents behind the novel objects, smells, sounds and activities that keep animals active, interested and engaged year-round. Species throughout the Zoo will receive exciting surprises in their habitats, while human guests enjoy special Keeper Talks and training demos, crafts, character meet-and-greets, and themed activities.

Some of our favorite animal-inspired activities even allow you to test out your own abilities!

  • Forage like a gorilla!
  • Catch “bugs” like a kori bustard!
  • Forge like a sun bear!

Activities will run from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m . Free for Central Zoo Members and children under 3; free with general admission.
