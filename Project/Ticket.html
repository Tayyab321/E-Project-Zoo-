Before purchasing your tickets, please review our new safety guidelines here


All guests visiting the Central Zoo who are not members, ages two and older, are required to have advance tickets. In an effort to maintain physical distancing among guests, their will be limiting capacity within the Zoo over the 125 acres. A limited number of general admission tickets will be available at the gate each day with pricing of $19 per child or senior, and $23 per adult.

Central Zoo Safety Guidlines & Updates

Our goal is to provide a great Zoo experience in an environment that is as safe as possible for all. To maintain the health and safety of our guests, staff and endangered animals, there are a few safety guidelines and updates we would like you to review before your arrival.

MASK POLICY

Face masks are encouraged indoors for the well-being of each other, staff and the animals who call the Zoo home.

YOUR MEMBERSHIP

Central Zoological Society members are no longer required to book reservations to visit the Zoo during daytime hours. If you would like to purchase or renew a membership, click here

AS YOU ARRIVE

  • Parking is available in the surface lots and the parking structure.
  • Please show your ID and membership card (if applicable) before presenting your digital or printed timed ticket to the booth attendant, who will scan it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to wear a face mask when I visit?

  • Face masks are encouraged indoors for the well-being of each other, staff and the animals who call the Zoo home.Therefore face mask is necessary for all the people

Do I have to show up for my selected time and date slot?

Visitors purchasing general admission tickets will be required to pay a change fee per ticket to visit the Zoo on a different date.

How do I cancel my tickets?

All ticket purchases are non-refundable. Visitors purchasing general admission tickets will be required to pay a change fee per ticket to visit the Zoo on a different date.

Can I bring my dog/pet?

For the safety of your pet and our animals, pets are not allowed on Zoo grounds.
