Zoo Rules

  • Respect the Animals. Please treat the Zoo's animals with the respect they deserve

  • Respect your Boundaries. Never cross any fence or barrier in the Zoo

  • Do Not Feed the Animals

  • No Smoking.

  • No Pets

  • Children Must be Supervised at All Times

  • Dress Code

  • Entrance to zoo is possible only with a valid ticket

  • Entrance of dogs is allowed with a person older than 15 years after a valid ticket purchase. A dog had to be on leash during visit

  • Entrance of children under 10 years is allowed only with a person older than 15 years.

  • A leader of mass excursion is responsible for whole group safety. For a children safety is responsible their accompanist

Forbidden Things/Elements

  • Using record players, radios, musical instruments, horns and other similar appliances

  • Bringing pyrotechnics, inflatable balloons and other items which can jeopardize safety or health of visitors and animals

  • Entering areas outside the pathways for visitors and other areas restricted to the public

  • Entry beyond barriers near exhibits

  • Feeding of animals, disturbing or irritation of animals

  • Picking and destroying floral and tree decoration throughout the entire ZOO

  • Smoking (smoking is permited only in reserved places)
