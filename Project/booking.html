GENERAL ADMISSION TICKET RESERVATIONS

Buy Early and Save !


All guests visiting the Detroit Zoo, ages two and older, are required to have advance tickets to guarantee a visit to the Detroit Zoo.

In an effort to maintain physical distancing among guests, the DZS will be limiting capacity within the Zoo over the 125 acres. A limited number of general admission tickets will be available at the gate each day with pricing of $19 per child or senior, and $23 per adult. The best way to guarantee your Detroit Zoo visit is to book your tickets in advance online.

The Detroit Zoological Society has revised safety protocols. Regardless of vaccination status, face masks are required indoors for people age five and older. It is our goal to make the Zoo experience one that is safe for our visitors, staff and animals. Let’s work together. Please be respectful, help each other and help protect the endangered animals.

The Zoo will open daily from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Please arrive on time for your reservation to ensure entry. Those who arrive late may be required to wait for the next available entry time.

Don't forget to purchase your parking pass! Parking passes must be purchased in advance for a fee of $8 per vehicle. You will present your parking pass at the main admission gate for validation; do not leave it in your vehicle.
